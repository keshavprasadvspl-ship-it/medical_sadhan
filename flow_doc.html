Detailed Process Flow

1
Registration & Onboarding

BuyerHospitals, medical stores sign up via app/web

VendorDistributors sign up and connect accounting software (Tally/ERP)

AdminManages registrations and verifies accounts

2
Product Search & Selection

BuyerLogs in and searches products by name, brand, composition, category, vendor, price

Views detailed product info: batch, expiry, GST, stock, price. Compares vendors for same product.

3
Order Creation & Checkout

BuyerAdds products to cart, selects preferred vendor

Confirms order with GST details, delivery address, quantities. Order generated instantly.

4
Vendor Order Processing

Order auto-pushed to vendor's software via API, XML/JSON, Excel import, or sync

VendorReceives alert in app, accepts/rejects order

Updates dispatch status and generates invoice. No manual entry required.

5
Notification & Tracking

BuyerGets notifications for order accepted, dispatched, invoice ready

VendorTracks order status and payment. Can download invoice.

6
Admin Oversight & Reporting

AdminMonitors all orders, users, and integrations

Generates reports, manages commissions, ensures system health through web admin panel.
